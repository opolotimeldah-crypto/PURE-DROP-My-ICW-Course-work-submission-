About Pure Drop

Pure Drop is a student-led initiative committed to improving access to safe and clean water in underprivileged communities. We believe that clean water is not just a basic need it’s a right.

Through community partnerships, awareness campaigns, and hands on projects, we’re changing lives one drop at a time. Join us in creating a future where everyone has access to pure, safe water.
